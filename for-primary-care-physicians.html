Services for Primary Care Physicians.

PCS created a comprehensive network of Primary Care and Specialist Physicians for complete Patient Care Coordination. Through this network you will be able to offer the best possible care to your patients and reduce administrative workloads on your staff. By joining the PCS network, you will be able to offer your patients a higher level of care, providing our Patient Concierge services free of charge and freeing more of your and your staff’s time to focus on the more important tasks.

PCS fulfills the increasing need for patient care coordination within the healthcare system, by streamlining the specialist location and appointment process for the patient. We take on the tasks, that you and your staff simply do not have the time for, covering the entire administrative cycle of the patient care process from identifying appropriate matching specialists for the patient, assisting with setting the appointment, coordinating with the insurance carrier, confirming pre-authorizations, following up and the forwarding of the results to you in a timely and professional manner. Our trained professionals are dedicated to effectively manage the entire specialist visit cycle, with years of relevant experience and desire to provide the highest-quality service to your patients, Primary Care Providers and Specialists, by relieving them of the unnecessary administrative stress.

Some of the key services we provide:

  • finding specialists based on location and carrier criteria
  • scheduling and confirming appointments
  • ensuring pre-authorizations
  • performing follow ups with specialists and/or their staff
  • overseeing forwarding of results to the Primary Care Provider
  • patient support via telephone or email
  • extended hours of operation
  • trained and experienced professional staff that is ready to serve you and your
