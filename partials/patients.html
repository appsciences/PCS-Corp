What is a Patient Concierge?

Patient Concierge is the answer to the question posted by the voids created inherently in the modern healthcare system. After decades of providing quality primary and urgent health care services to millions of patients, founders of PCS set out to provide Patient Concierge service to everyone. For free! You may think of us as the personal secretary with lots of healthcare industry experience, who will bridge the gap between your primary healthcare provider and any specialist you may have to visit in the course of your treatment.

PCS works hard to fulfill these integral requirements for patients, who require the help of a specialist doctor:

  • finding specialist doctor in network with your insurance
  • finding a specialist convenient to your location
  • appointment scheduling and confirmation streamlining
  • follow-up with the specialist, ensuring your primary physician receives the results in
    • a timely and professional manner
  • expanding the number of options for the patients in terms of finding the right specialist
  • extended hours of phone and email support to patients
  • practical information regarding getting the most out of your specialist visit
  • trained professional staff, with decades of healthcare and patient care coordination experience
  • expert handling of the entire specialist visit process on behalf of the patient

Here’s a statistic that no one would ever want to be a part of:

“Advocates for hospital patients and their families say confusion about who is managing a patient’s care — and lack of coordination among those caregivers — are endemic, contributing to the estimated 44,000 to 98,000 deaths from medical errors each year.”

(Kaiserhealthnews.org, April 30, 2013)

Since then, major hospitals have increased the hiring and training of Patient Care Coordinators - professionally trained staff that oversees the entire treatment cycle. However, smaller Primary Care (general) practices simply cannot afford to hire such professionals, and their existing staffs - however well-trained they may be - often simply do not have the time to process all the administrative work, required for specialist visits. This effectively leaves the responsibility for finding the specialist and coordinating the visit in the hands of the patient and/or patient’s family. We believe this be an unfair burden and have created PCS to assist patients everywhere in getting the most out of their specialist visits, while freeing them up to focus solely on treatment and recovery.
